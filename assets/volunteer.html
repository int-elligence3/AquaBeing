Why Volunteer for us?

“The Earth is what we all have in common.” With the increasing rate of industrialization, wastes and pollution are embedding their feet on earth. Due to this, many diseases are arising at a tremendous rate. But what humans can see is the growth of societies and not of pollution and degradation of the earth. So, in order to eradicate pollution and save our earth, it's time to come together and take action. Through 'AquaBeing', we want to give a small contribution towards our mother earth and employment to people so that we can solve two problems together. We welcome those who want to help us in this cause, we’d be really grateful if you could donate and help us in expanding our vision. The funds that we’ll get, would help us to reward our volunteers and hence inspire them further for doing good deeds. Devote yourselves towards cleaning water bodies and play your part in nurturing nature! Volunteer now!
